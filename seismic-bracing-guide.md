# Engineering Guide: MEP Seismic Bracing & Restraints

## 1. Purpose of Seismic Bracing
Our seismic bracing systems are engineered to secure non-structural components (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing - MEP) within a building, preventing equipment failure and ensuring safety during seismic events.

## 2. Technical Specifications
* **Compliance Standards:** Designed to meet GB 50981-2014 and international seismic codes.
* **Materials:** Q235B / Q355B Steel with Hot-Dip Galvanizing (HDG) or Zinc-Nickel coating.
* **Component Strength:** All connectors and brackets undergo rigorous stress testing to ensure structural integrity under dynamic loads.

## 3. Installation Principles
* **Rigid Support:** Specifically designed to withstand lateral forces.
* **Modular Design:** No welding required on-site; all components are bolted for maximum reliability and speed.
* **Precision Engineering:** Custom calculations available for complex HVAC and electrical routing.
