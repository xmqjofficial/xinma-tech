# Tech Specs: Molded Corrugated Bottom Cable Tray

## 1. Product Overview
The molded corrugated bottom design is a patented innovation by Shanghai Xinma. By altering the physical geometry of the tray bottom, we achieve superior structural rigidity without adding excess metal weight.

## 2. Technical Parameters
* **Material Options:** Hot-Dip Galvanized Steel, Aluminum Alloy, Stainless Steel (304/316L)
* **Standard Length:** 2000mm / 3000mm / 6000mm
* **Width Range:** 100mm to 1000mm
* **Load Capacity:** Up to 30% higher than traditional flat-bottom trays of the same thickness.

## 3. Key Advantages
* **Optimized Heat Dissipation:** The corrugated profile increases surface area, allowing power cables to remain cooler under heavy loads.
* **Faster Installation:** Lighter unit weight reduces labor fatigue and equipment requirements on-site.
