# Technical Data: Heavy-Duty Busway Systems

## 1. System Overview
Xinma Busway Systems are designed for high-efficiency power distribution in large-scale industrial plants, commercial complexes, and data centers. Our compact design ensures minimal voltage drop and maximum space utilization.

## 2. Load & Performance Parameters
* **Current Rating:** 250A to 6300A
* **Voltage Rating:** Up to 1000V
* **IP Rating:** IP54 / IP65 / IP68 (Customizable for harsh environments)
* **Temperature Rise:** Strictly compliant with GB/T 7251.6 and IEC 61439-6 standards.

## 3. Structural Features
* **Compact Sandwich Structure:** Provides excellent heat dissipation and high short-circuit strength.
* **Corrosion Resistance:** High-strength aluminum alloy or galvanized steel housing with epoxy resin coating.
* **Easy Installation:** Plug-in design reduces site labor costs by up to 40%.
